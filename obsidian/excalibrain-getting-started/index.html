Issue-Based Information System

IBIS(基于问题的信息系统)框架，它是一种对话映射方法，包括问题、立场、支持和反对论点，以及证据和结论。

  • Issues: phrased as questions, drives a conversation about issues
  • Positions: 相应问题时对应的立场
  • Supporting and Objecting Arguments: support or object positions
  • Evidences: 证据，attach to various nodes reaching a conclusion
  • Conclusion
